Kermis colorscheme

Constructing this colorscheme, Solarized colorscheme was a model: sixteen colors in aestheticaly logic coherence. Like in Solarized, the colorscheme comes both in a light – and in a dark background version, and because the colornames are the same as in Solarized, it’s quite easy to do a “search and replace” in a texteditor, and swap the colors (when converting any copy of a Solarized theme file …).

Starting point was the idea to have brownish colors for the background, not unlike a colorsheme like “Birds of Paradise” by Joe Bergantine, but a bit muddier on the browns, less red and more greyed out, like in clay. The name “Kermis” comes from the Dutch word for “fair”, or “funfair”.
